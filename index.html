Visualising Tax in Ethiopia

A quick look at some of the highlights surrounding taxation of Ethiopia's imports.

Ethiopia's imports rose by 668% in 17 years

In 1997, Ethiopia was importing just over 20,000 shipments** per year. By 2013 that number ballooned to over 160,000, indicating a growth of 668% between 1997 and 2013.

The Top 5 Most Frequently Imported Items of 2013 (by HS Code)

  1. Gaskets, washers and other seals, of vulcanized rubber (40169300)
  2. Screws and bolts of iron or steel, nes (73181500)
  3. Parts and accessories, nes, for vehicles of 87.01 to 87.05 (87089900)
  4. --- OTHER ARTICLS OF PLASTICS, NES (39269090)
  5. Other appliances such as taps, cocks and other valves, nes (84818000)

* "nes" = not elsewhere specified

Where did 2013's imports come from?

China was the #1 origin of all imports with 43,975 imports in all (representing 27.5% of all imports in Ethiopia) By comparison, the #2 origin country (Italy) was the source of only 13,152 imports (8.22% of all imports in 2013)

In fact, the number of imports from China alone amount to almost all of the next 4 countries combined (Italy, India, USA, and Germany)
